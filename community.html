Join the Community

Ask questions, share your progress, and learn alongside others making their way into bioinformatics. No question is too basic here.

How it works

Our discussion board is powered by GitHub, so your conversations are safe, searchable, and spam-free. Getting started takes a minute.

1

Sign in with GitHub

Use a free GitHub account to post — no separate sign-up. New to GitHub? Creating an account takes under two minutes.

2

Post or reply

Ask a question, introduce yourself, or share a win. Add code, screenshots, and links — formatting is supported.

3

Learn together

Get answers from fellow learners and the BioNexus team, and help others when you can. React, follow up, and grow.

Community guidelines

  • Be kind and welcoming. Everyone here is learning — including people switching careers and writing their first line of code. Treat every question with respect.
  • There are no "dumb" questions. If you're stuck, someone else is too. Asking helps the whole community.
  • Search before posting. A quick look may surface an existing answer — and it keeps the board tidy.
  • Share enough detail. Include what you tried, the exact error, and your code or data when asking for help. It gets you better answers, faster.
  • Pay it forward. Answer a question when you can. Teaching is one of the best ways to learn.
  • No spam or self-promotion unrelated to learning bioinformatics.
